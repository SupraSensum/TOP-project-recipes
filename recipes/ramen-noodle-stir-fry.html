Ramen Noodle Stir Fry

Squiggly foray into noodle land

He had a severe case of ramen noodle stir-fry addiction. He would spend all his money and time on buying and making the dish. He would sneak into restaurants and steal their ingredients. He would break into people's homes and use their kitchens. He would even rob banks and jewelry stores to fund his habit. He did not care about the law, his health, or his family. He only cared about getting his fix of ramen noodle stir-fry. He was a menace to society and a danger to himself.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of instant ramen noodles (any flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped carrot
  • 1/4 cup of chopped broccoli
  • 1/4 cup of cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package directions, but do not add the seasoning packet. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion, carrot and broccoli and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until tender.
  3. Add the chicken and soy sauce and stir to combine. Cook for another 5 minutes, until heated through.
  4. Add the cooked noodles and toss to coat with the sauce. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
  5. Enjoy!
