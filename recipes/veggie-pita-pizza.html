Veggie Pita Pizza

Decepti-pita pizza comes to sabotage once again

I was looking forward to a delicious Veggie Pita Pizza for dinner, but what I got was a disappointing mess. The pita bread was dry and crumbly, the cheese was barely melted, and the veggies were bland and soggy. The sauce was too sweet and had a weird aftertaste. It was not worth the money or the calories. I ended up throwing most of it away and eating some cereal instead.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole wheat pita bread
  • 2 tablespoons of pizza sauce (or tomato paste, salsa, pesto etc.)
  • 1/4 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup of sliced bell pepper
  • Salt, pepper and oregano to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Place the pita bread on the prepared baking sheet and spread with pizza sauce evenly.
  3. Sprinkle cheese if using and top with mushrooms, olives and bell pepper. Season with salt, pepper and oregano as desired.
  4. Bake for about 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and pita is crisp.
  5. Cut into wedges and enjoy!
